This is the most ingenious part of radio physics. There's a huge difference between the satellite antenna and the ground antenna.

The G-MASTER/G-NODE (Funnel): Since the satellite signal arrives at the ground "scattered" and weak, the ground antenna must be a high-gain parabolic dish or Yagi. These antennas work like enormous funnels: they collect the weak signal from a large area and concentrate it all in the receiver.
